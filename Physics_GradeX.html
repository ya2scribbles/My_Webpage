Grade X Physics

Physics is one of the subject that student will learn when they got into highschool. For grade X, usually it start with introduction to Physics chapter. After that continue to basic of mechanics.
Hence, these are chapter that will we learn for grade X:

  1. Quantity and Unit
  2. Rule of writing Number
  3. measurement
  4. Dimension
  5. Concept of Vector
  6. Operation of Vector
  7. Kinematics of Linear Motion
  8. Kinematics of Circular Motion
  9. Projectile Motion
  10. Dinamics of Linear Motion
  11. Law of Newton's Gravity
  12. Work and Energy
  13. Conservation of Energy
  14. Momentum and Impuls
  15. Simple Oscillation Motion

As you can see, focus of physics on grade X is to familiarize student with early physical concepts.
Such us measurement, how to read quantity, and the property of motion. These are the reason why physics become mandatory subject for highschool student.

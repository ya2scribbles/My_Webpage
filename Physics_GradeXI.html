Grade XI Physics

The continuation of learning physics will bring you to understand the mechamism behind engineering which we found on daily basis. that's why the first thing that will introduce to you are equilibrium rigid body which is very helpful when we try do build something like house, shop, or another building. after that continue with another daily phenomena like elasticity, fluids, gasses, heat, and wave.
Hence for grade XI physics we will learn those chapter:

  1. Equilibrium of Rigid Body
  2. Centre of Mass
  3. Dynamic of Rotational Motion
  4. Elasticity
  5. Static Fluids
  6. Fluid Dynamics
  7. Heat and Temperature
  8. Kinematic Theory of Gasses
  9. Thermodynamics
  10. Mechanical Wave
  11. Sound Wave
  12. Light Wave
  13. Optical Geometry

As you can see, focus of physics on grade XI is to familiarize student with engineering physics and natural law of matter.
The goal of these chapter is to make study have understanding of physics that surrounding our daily life. These are the reason why these chapter be put on grade XI for highschool student.

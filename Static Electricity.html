Static Electricity

Concept of Electric Static

Try to rub your plastic ruler to your hair, after that move it toward scrap of paper. We could see scrap of paper would fly toward ruler. This is the example of static electricity. On daily activity level, each of object usually has neutral charge. The positive charge happened when object lose a few negative particle, while negative charge happened when object gain a few negative particle. When plastic ruler rub to your hair, it gains some negative particle. Hence it will attract scrap of paper whose more positive than ruler. That’s what happened in daily phenomenon. But on smaller level, it’s different story. And the negative particle that could freely move between object are known as electron.

We already establish that every matter in the world made by atoms. And atoms made by proton, neutrons, and electron. The composition of proton made element that we know in periodic table, the composition electron made ion that we know today, and composition of neutron made isotop of an element. Proton and neutron are the core of atoms, meanwhile electron orbit around the core of atoms. Proton has positive charge, electron has negative charge, and neutron has neutral. Hence in summary, information about component of atoms could be see in table below :

We established that different type of charge would attract of each other, meanwhile same type of charge would repel of each other. Meanwhile neutral object does not affect by charge. To understand why it’s happen, it’s because each charge has electric field. Electric field are field that would affect things around charge.

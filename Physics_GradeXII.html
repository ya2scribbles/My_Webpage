Grade XII Physics

In Grade XII we'll explore electricity, magnetism, and modern physics topics that prepare students for university-level physics. The course includes both classical electrodynamics and introductions to atomic, quantum, and nuclear physics.

For Grade XII we will study these chapters:

  1. Static Electricity
  2. Electric Current
  3. Magnetism
  4. Electromagnetism
  5. Alternating Current
  6. Atomic Physics
  7. Quantum Theory
  8. Relativity
  9. Radioactivity
  10. Digital Technology

The emphasis is on building conceptual understanding and mathematical descriptions of fields, waves, and particles so students can connect classical and modern views of physics before graduation.
